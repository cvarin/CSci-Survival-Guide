About the author

I'm a computational physicist. It means I work with computers all the time, from laptops to multi-million-dollar simulation clusters. Honestly, I don't know much about computer science, but I know how to do science with computers. I'm a convinced open-source advocate and I fell like I explain the same things over and over again to my colleagues, especially about getting started with Python, git, and SSH. This is why I created this series of articles.
