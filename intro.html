Introduction

Thu 13 November 2014

If you end up here, you are probably involved with computer simulations of some sort. If you are about to spend hours in front of a computer, why not making it enjoyable? Why not getting rid of expensive, overkill, licensed software you do not need? You will see, working with open source software makes it nice, whith the feeling that you are in control of what you are doing.

In this guide, I share my survival tricks: simple methods and techniques that you can always rely on. Of course, there are situations where more complex approaches are needed. But when in doubt, you still have these simple codes you know and understand very well, to compare and make sure everything is right.

I hope you will find the upcoming articles interesting and useful!
